One Ruler to Rule Them All.

A ruler with a reference sheet for dimensions of electronic components.
          Anyone who has worked with electronic components knows how difficult it can get to keep track of the sizes of the various components and how to place them on your board. God forbid you make any mistake in the layout of your PCB board because that spells nothing other than "START AGAIN" for you and your team that may have been working round the clock to design the circuit. The electronic components that can be used on the board come in all shapes and sizes, and trying to memorize their dimensions is just futile because there are so-goddamn-many of them. Now you may think,

"If only there was something that would show all the common components at a glance for easy reference!"

Well, here it is. The PCB Ruler - A ruler made using the same material (and process!) that one would use for manufacturing PCBs, but this one contains the outline of common electronic components printed onto the ruler itself!
It was designed and developed by Aditya R. Mhatre who introduces this tool as follows:
A PCB Ruler is the PCB Design reference tool to refer/measuring the various parameters utilised by the hardware layout engineer while designing PCB. The "PCB Ruler itself is a PCB" designed in such way that it will provide the physical understanding of the multiple parameters of the layout like Size of trace, Size of Vias, Size of Drill Holes, Different Passive Component Packages, General ruler for the measurement, PAD pitch etc.
This tool was developed with 4 points in mind:
  1. To understand the layout designing parameters physically, before fabrication.
  2. To cover maximum possible parameters in compact and handy PCB Size/Shape.
  3. To include a measuring scale, protractor for the angle measurement and the electrical parameters relation as per the Ohm’s Law.
  4. To make this a 100% "Made In India" product, as it is Conceptualised, Designed, Developed and Fabricated within the country.
